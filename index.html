What is a Taco Mafia?



Taco Mafia is a clan of PC gamers that play video games on a regular basis. While Monday is the official game night, our growing clan of casualcore gamers plays every day. Beginning as a small group of St. Louis based Day Z players, we have now expanded to over 50 players worldwide on PC, Xbox, and PS4. We have a teamspeak server where there is almost always someone online.

We're very against hacking, cheating, and duping, and most players are over the age of 25 and based in the midwest USA. Yes, we also partake in Taco Tuesday. If you'd like to join, send an e-mail using the contact form at the bottom of the page.
